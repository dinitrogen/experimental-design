


| Approach (IV) | DV | Quality | Notes |
|:--------------|:---|:-------:|:------|
| **Canopy diameter/area** (vary size, constant payload & drop height) | Drop time (sec) | ✅ Best option | Most intuitive; clear relationship; easy to control |
| **String length** (vary length, constant canopy & payload) | Drop time (sec) | ✅ Good | Works — shorter strings bunch canopy, longer strings let it open fully |
| **Payload mass** (vary # of binder clips, constant canopy) | Drop time (sec) | ⚠️ Okay | Requires multiple clips; trend is real but less dramatic |
| **Canopy shape** (circle vs. square vs. triangle) | Drop time (sec) | ❌ Poor | Categorical IV with only 3 levels; hard to see a trend or graph meaningfully |
| **Drop height** | Drop time (sec) | ⚠️ Okay | Real relationship but less about parachute *design*; less relevant to the scenario |

### Approach A: Vary Canopy Diameter (constant payload = 1 binder clip, drop height = 1.5 m)

**Best-case IV levels:** 10 cm, 20 cm, 30 cm, 40 cm

| Canopy Diameter (cm) | Trial 1 (sec) | Trial 2 (sec) | Trial 3 (sec) | Mean (sec) |
|:---------------------:|:--------------:|:--------------:|:--------------:|:----------:|
| 10 | 0.82 | 0.78 | 0.85 | 0.82 |
| 20 | 1.14 | 1.21 | 1.08 | 1.14 |
| 30 | 1.63 | 1.71 | 1.55 | 1.63 |
| 40 | 2.24 | 2.35 | 2.18 | 2.26 |

- **Trend:** Positive correlation — larger canopy → longer (slower) drop time
- **Std dev:** Increases slightly with canopy size (~0.03–0.09 sec)
- **No outliers** in this data set

If a team uses only **3 IV levels**, provide only the first three rows (10, 20, 30 cm) or let them choose which three.

### Approach B: Vary String Length (constant canopy = 25 cm diameter, payload = 1 binder clip, drop height = 1.5 m)

**Best-case IV levels:** 5 cm, 15 cm, 25 cm

| String Length (cm) | Trial 1 (sec) | Trial 2 (sec) | Trial 3 (sec) | Mean (sec) |
|:------------------:|:--------------:|:--------------:|:--------------:|:----------:|
| 5 | 1.05 | 0.98 | 1.10 | 1.04 |
| 15 | 1.38 | 1.42 | 1.31 | 1.37 |
| 25 | 1.54 | 1.61 | 1.49 | 1.55 |

- **Trend:** Positive correlation — longer strings → slightly longer drop time (canopy opens more fully)
- **Note:** The effect is less dramatic than canopy size; the trend flattens at longer lengths
- **Std dev:** ~0.05–0.06 sec across levels

### Approach C: Vary Payload Mass (constant canopy = 25 cm diameter, string = 15 cm, drop height = 1.5 m)

**Best-case IV levels:** 1 clip (~10 g), 2 clips (~20 g), 3 clips (~30 g)

| Payload (# of clips) | Trial 1 (sec) | Trial 2 (sec) | Trial 3 (sec) | Mean (sec) |
|:---------------------:|:--------------:|:--------------:|:--------------:|:----------:|
| 1 (~10 g) | 1.52 | 1.47 | 1.58 | 1.52 |
| 2 (~20 g) | 1.21 | 1.28 | 1.18 | 1.22 |
| 3 (~30 g) | 1.02 | 0.95 | 1.08 | 1.02 |

- **Trend:** Negative correlation — heavier payload → faster (shorter) drop time
- **Std dev:** ~0.05–0.07 sec across levels
