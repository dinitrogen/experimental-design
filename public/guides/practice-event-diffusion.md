## Experimental Prompt

Design and conduct an experiment to investigate a factor that affects the rate of diffusion.

---

## Background Information

You are researchers at a pharmaceutical company developing a new dissolvable medication tablet. Your team needs to understand how substances spread through liquids — a process called **diffusion**. Before you can optimize the tablet design, you need to investigate the factors that affect how quickly a substance diffuses through a fluid.

**Diffusion** is the movement of particles from an area of high concentration to an area of low concentration. This process occurs naturally and does not require any external force — it is driven by the random motion of molecules. Factors such as **temperature**, **concentration**, and **particle size** can all influence how quickly diffusion occurs.

Your experiment's results and conclusions should help your research team **make informed decisions about how to design a tablet that dissolves and diffuses effectively.**

**Important notes:**

- You must use **at least 2** of the provided materials in your experiment.
- Your experiment must address the given topic of **diffusion**.

**Tip:** 3 trials per IV level is sufficient — plan your time wisely!
