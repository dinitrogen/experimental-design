## Experimental Prompt

Design an experiment to investigate a factor that affects the drop time of a parachute.

---

## Background Information

You are engineers working for an aerospace supply company. Your team is developing a parachute system to safely airdrop supply packages to remote locations. Before finalizing the design, you need to understand how different aspects of a parachute affect its performance. Your experiment's results and conclusions should help your engineering team **make informed decisions about their parachute design.**

A parachute has several features that could be changed — its canopy, its strings, the weight it carries, and how it is dropped. Your job is to choose **one factor** to investigate and design a controlled experiment around it.


## Materials List (use at least 3)

- Plastic trash bags (for canopy material)
- String (pre-cut to 30 cm lengths)
- Scissors
- Tape
- Small binder clips (as payload — ~10 g each)
- Meterstick
- Hole punch

#### You may also use your stopwatch, ruler, and calculator.
